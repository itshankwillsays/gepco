How to Check Your GEPCO Electricity Bill Online

To check your GEPCO electricity bill online, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Locate Your Reference Number

Before you begin, you need your 14-digit reference number, which can be found on your previous bill. It's usually located at the top left corner or just below the Customer ID.

Step 2: Visit the GEPCO Online Bill Check Portal

Go to the official GEPCO website or a reliable third-party site that offers bill-checking services such as https://gepcoonlinebill.com.pk/

Step 3: Enter Your Reference Number

In the designated field, enter your reference number without any spaces or hyphens. This is crucial for accurate retrieval of your bill.

Step 4: Submit Your Information

Click on the "Check Bill" button. The system will process your request and display your latest bill details.

Step 5: Review Your Bill

Once the bill is displayed, you can view important information such as:

  • Total amount due
  • Units consumed
  • Reading date
  • Due date

Step 6: Download or Print Your Bill

For record-keeping, you can download the bill in PDF format or print it directly from the website. This ensures you have a copy for future reference and payment.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can I check my GEPCO bill online?

To check your GEPCO bill online, visit the official GEPCO website or a trusted portal, enter your 14-digit reference number, and click "Check Bill."

What if I don't have my reference number?

Unfortunately, you need the reference number to access your bill online. It is essential for identifying your account.

Can I pay my GEPCO bill online?

Yes! After checking your bill, you can pay it through various online payment methods available on the GEPCO website.

What should I do if I encounter issues while checking my bill?

If you face difficulties accessing your bill, contact GEPCO customer service for assistance or visit their nearest service center.

Are there any charges for checking my GEPCO bill online?

No, checking your GEPCO bill online is completely free of charge.
